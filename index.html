How to Use This Tracker

1
Go to Weekly Entry and select the current week tab.
2
Complete your row before noon on Monday. Row not completed on time → values automatically set to 0.
3
Joel and Marc-André will also complete their rows each week as management.
4
Data auto-saves. Use Export/Import to move between devices.

Columns to Fill In

→
Field Days — Number of full days in the field this week. Target: 3–4.
→
Confirmed Visits — Total visits completed. Target: 4–6 per field day.
→
New Appts Booked — Appointments scheduled for upcoming weeks.
→
CRM Deals Created — New opportunities opened in the CRM this week.
→
Notes — Obstacles, opportunities, useful observations.

Minimum Standards (Priority KPIs)

✔3 to 4 field days per week
✔4 to 6 confirmed visits per field day
✔Calendar showing 7–10 days of reserved activity at all times
✔Route plan submitted every Monday morning
